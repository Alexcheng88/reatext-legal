ReaText Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2025-05-27

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account data (if any): none (no registration required)
  • Text recognition results saved in history (stored locally on your device)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device info: IP address, operating system, app version
  • Usage data: features used and timestamps

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this data to improve OCR accuracy, enable history lookup, and diagnose issues.

3. Data Retention

History entries are kept until you delete them manually; we do not retain any data on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

ReaText does not share any personal data with third parties. All processing occurs locally.

5. Security

All data is stored on your device; ReaText does not transmit or store data on remote servers.

6. Children’s Privacy

ReaText is not intended for children under 13 years old.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy; changes will appear here with a new Effective Date.
